## Commercial clusters

### 1. Broad Philippines outsourcing

Primary terms: `outsourcing philippines`, `philippines outsourcing`, `outsource philippines`, `outsourcing to philippines`, and `outsource to philippines`.

Buyer job: decide whether the Philippines fits, what work to move, and how to compare a staffing partner. The broad guide is the hub for the whole plan.

### 2. Customer and contact center

Primary terms: `contact center outsourcing` and `call center outsourcing philippines`.

Buyer job: compare channels, coverage, quality checks, escalation rules, and launch steps. Link this cluster to customer support and reporting/QA services.

### 3. Technology and product delivery

Primary terms: `IT outsourcing philippines`, `outsourced product development`, and `outsource web development philippines`.

Buyer job: choose between staff support and a project vendor, set access, protect repositories, and review delivery. Avoid claiming software capabilities until the offer can serve them.

### 4. Specialist staffing

Primary terms: `healthcare bpo philippines`, `virtual assistant in philippines`, `outsource SEO to philippines`, and `staff outsourcing philippines`.

Buyer job: define the role, tools, compliance boundary, work sample, scorecard, and replacement path. Each guide must state when specialist legal or security advice is needed.

### 5. Provider choice and commercial checks

Primary terms: `best outsourcing company`, `BPO outsourcing companies`, `philippines outsourcing companies`, and `outsourcing companies in the philippines`.

Buyer job: shortlist providers without relying on unsupported rankings. Use a scorecard, questions, contract checks, and visible selection rules. Do not publish pay-to-play ‚Äúbest‚Äù lists.
